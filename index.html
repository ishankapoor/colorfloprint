About Us


Started in 2018, ColorFlo Printech intends to set a new frontier in shade card quality. Our founder worked in color cards for 35 years before this new venture.

Originally the team behind ColorFlo was the first to bring in shade card manufacturing in India via partnership with Typolac of Germany in 1980.

With our deep understanding of fluid dynamics, chemistry and our advanced analytics based color matching technology the quality of our colors is unmatched in the Indian market.

We have been based in NOIDA, India and providing shade cards in various form factors for the Indian market.
